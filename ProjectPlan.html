Mission


Purpose of Website

SSBC 2018 is established for the following purpose:

  • To encourage researchers, students, or whoever is interested in AI or machine learning to join their competition.
  • To provide user-friendly navigation when browsing throughout the entire website.
  • To provide additional elements such as news and results to be eye catching

Success Evaluation

Description

Key performance indicators are metrics that can be used to quantitatively measure SSBC's website's success.
More general web analytics can be studied for an indication of the general performance of the website established. Such examples of softwares would be Google Analytics which can be used to analyze data such as:

  • Volume of visitors to the website
  • Geographical location of visitors
  • Devices/ operating systems that visitors use
  • Where visitors will be directed to and from
  • How users navigate throughout the website
  • The popularity of different pages
  • The conversion rate of the website
In addition, bots and spiders are filtered out from viewable data by Google Analytics.
A cache copy of the old website could be kept as a reference after the redeveloped website goes live.
Any increase in performance or sales after the replacement will be credited to the establishment of the new SSBC's website.
